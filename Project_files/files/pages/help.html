Instructions

        The website gives you a few options for filling out your invoice. You can look at the invoice preview on the right side
        to make sure all the information are in the place you want them to be. If the input field are not enough to get your desired
        setup, you can use the Direct Edit button, and manually edit the preview. It is better to turn off the direct edit
        mode when you are not using it to avoid unwanted changes.

How to use:

        You can fill out the input boxes on the left side and click on the Update Preview button to update the invoice preview
        on the right side.
        Add or remove items with the Add Item and Remove Item buttons.
        In order to fill in your items, you can use the Direct Edit mode and fill in the items directly.
        After filling out your items, you can click on the Calculate and the program will calculate the total.
